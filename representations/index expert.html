The Study of Representations: Biological Processes

Dear participant, thank you very much for participating in our study investigating the representations of biological processes.

The information from this study will be used as part of a PhD research project by Hana Pokojna. If you have any further questions, please contact her at xpokojna@fi.muni.cz.


The questionnaire consists of six parts. The first part takes place on this page and collects information about you. The remaining five parts each show one representation of a biological process. You will be asked to view all five of the representations and answer questions related to them and their effectiveness. We expect the study to last up to 60 minutes. Answering all the questions is mandatory. We recommend to complete this study on a screen with at least 1024 x 768 resolution. Please do not close the windows or reload the pages while taking part in the study. If you are under 18, please do not take part in this study.
